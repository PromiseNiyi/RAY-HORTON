RAY'S JOURNEY

Just a kid from Tacoma, Washington. Ray grew up the 4th of 5 children of Albert and Josie Horton. Albert was a proud member of the U.S. Army and served our country honorably in WWII.

Ray started his wealth building journey as a child by delivering newspapers at 6 in the morning and then later in life at the age of 15, as a dishwasher in a chinese restaurant after school until 11 p.m., then walking the 2 miles home.
